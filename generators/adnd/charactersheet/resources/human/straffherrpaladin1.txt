“Citizen, I charge you with theft, conspiracy and murder in the first degree. Allow me to also express my personal disgust as you didn't even have the common decency to bury the body… litter bug.”

You gain Rope Use and Wrestling in addition to your skills. Any humanoid can be captured alive and tied up. A clergy man will go to the site and collect the criminal, giving you the bounty. You gain 1 Iron per level of opponent. x1 for men-at-arms, x5 for henchmen, x10 for heroes, x100 for lair bosses. Gain an additional x2 for bringing the criminal into town yourself.

Don't forget to write up a citation.
