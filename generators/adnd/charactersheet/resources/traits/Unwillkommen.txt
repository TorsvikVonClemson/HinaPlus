~~~Unwillkommen~~~

"The creatures that stalk us after sunfall. They abandoned their humanity. They are no longer welcome here"

-Saint Leopold Hobbach, The Blessing of Trogdenheim. Jahr 27 von Schism Zweite

Once a day you may bless a 50 ft radius for an entire day. Any humans in this area have their weapons blessed with magic+1. Night creatures (including vampires, werewolves and trolls) cannot enter this area and must leave this area or be turned to ash.
