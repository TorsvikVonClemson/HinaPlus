Your standoffish demeanor has a powerful effect on Reaction Checks. For races that respect an abrasive personality (Orcs, Dwarves, etc) you can alter their current posture by 2 degrees. For timid races (Kobolds, Gobblins, etc) all successful attack rolls made against them cause a morale check.
