~~~An Unfortunate Constitution~~~

"There is no end to suffering but it always passes. Even Makabel never claimed that death was a box of birds."

-Missgescus der Realist. Excerpt from "die dreiKöpfige Münze"

You are blessed to the extraordinary ability to ignore wounds and pain. When you drop below 0 HP you do not fall unconscious. You will still die if you fall below -Con and you still must make bleed rolls until you are bandaged.
