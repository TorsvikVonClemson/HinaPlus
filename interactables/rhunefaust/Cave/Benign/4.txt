Guarded Agricultureal Treasure

Traveling through the tunnels you encounter a patch of subterranean vegitation you must traverse.

After inspecting the patch there is a small partitioned area. The small fence is made of rope and obviously attached to a trap. AGRICULTURE reveals the patch to be growing Sweet Pods. Integral crops to Dwarven Rum and Dwarven Sugar. 1-10 lbs at 100 gold per pound. The trap causes several spear to shoot up from the floor. 1-4 hit for long spear 2h dmg +1
