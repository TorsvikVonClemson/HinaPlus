Minor Rope Use Treasure

While Traveling through the woods the party is suprised as a wooden object falls from the sky. It is then slowly dragged across the ground and pulled back into the tree.

There is a wood elf in the tree, trying to make a bridge between two trees. He speaks some Faustic. Using ROPE USE you can aid him after which he will grant you hospitality and 2D6 days of food.
