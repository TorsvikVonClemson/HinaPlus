Etiquette Trap

In the distance you spot a 1d4+1 of wealthy nobles wandering and talking throught the woods.

Of the nobels only one is male, he introduces himself as a traveling merchant. He asks if there is a well mannered person who might help him. If someone attemps ETIQUETTE to help he will reveal himself as a Rakshasa and attempt to tear apart the player. If politely declines he will not persue. If the players are rude he will try harder to sway him to his side with promises of wealth. He does give of a magic aura to maintain his illusion.
