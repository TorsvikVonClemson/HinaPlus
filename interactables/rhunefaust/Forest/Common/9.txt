Human Bandit Subterfuge

The players hear the laughing and murmers of humans.

Roll up number of party heroes: They appear to be traveling nobles taking a break.

The nobles try to entice the players to come close.

After conversing with the nobles a barrage of arrows will rain down and the nobles will draw swords requesting that the party surrender.

Captured party members can be bought for 30 gold each.
