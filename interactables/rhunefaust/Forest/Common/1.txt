Swarm of Bats

Hearing check: The party hears a mass rustling of leaves or flapping of wings. Failure begins a sneak attack.

A massive swarm of bats descends on the party.

Bats: While in the swarm there is a 1% chance per pat to put out torches. Spellcaseters need to make a WIS check to cast spells and attack rolls take a -2.
